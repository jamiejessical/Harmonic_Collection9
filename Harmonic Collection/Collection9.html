To save time when cooking prawns, buy already deshelled prawns from your local grocery store.

*honest tip*

Microwave your prawns in a casserole dish, together with ginger and this sauce mixture: a teaspoon of soya sauce, a teaspoon of sesame oil and 3 teaspoons of water! (or you could just eyeball-it)
